The Main Experience

This interactive experience explores how digital accessibility barriers affect everyday tasks.

How it Works

  1. 1. Complete the Task

    Perform simple action (e.g., find contact information).

  2. 2. Experience the Barrier

    The interface will change to simulate an accessibility constraint.

  3. 3. Reflect and Learn

    Review how the experience differed and who is impacted.
